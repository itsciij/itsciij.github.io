Internship at Gengo

During the spring of 2015, I interned for Gengo, a translation startup. I worked closely with the creative team and our art director in designing marketing materials such as infographics, pdf guides, and illustrations.

Timeframe:
Feb-May 2015

Tasks:
Visual Design, Information Design, Illustration

Tools:
Illustrator, Photoshop, InDesign, Sketch

Social Media Guide

As an intern at Gengo, one of the fun tasks that was assigned to me was to design an online guide and take ownership of it. The content is all about social media and so I thought of a desk setup as its main theme. Following Gengo's brand guidelines, I played with my theme idea and added illustrations as visual cues. They also help make the content more inviting and engaging to read.





Illustrations

I also made a lot of fun illustrations for some of their web pages to make them more engaging.



Takeaways

It was a pleasure learning about how startups work, as well as being around optimistic and talented people. Having an experience to work in a fast-paced environment, this internship helped me in pacing myself and managing my time well. I was also able to learn how to work with grid-systems and typography for the web.
